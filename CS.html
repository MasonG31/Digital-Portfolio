Stone Soup

Another lesson that I learned while I was taking Computer Science I, was under the umbrella of the ancient European story of "Stone Soup". In this story a group of wandering travelers with only a bowl, are able to make a delicous bowl of soup for a town, by taking what they all individually had, carrots, water, celery, and make a large product in which they can share. This can apply to coding, and espically the difficult programs and software that we use in Computer Science. You don't need to be and expert in any of these fields, and you don't need to know all of the answers all of the time. You just need to be able to work with your group, large or small, and take little bits of information in what they know, and working together to make a great project.
